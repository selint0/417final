About Our Shop

We are a small shop located in Phoenix, Arizona with the belief that plants can bring people together. We also know how overwhelming entering the plant community can seem and we are always ready to help our customers no matter their starting level. We also firmly believe in giving back to the community through our sponsorships and free Friday classes. So come by and check us out!

Our Plant Products!

Indoor Friends

These plants all vary in care level and can either be a great addition to your space or a project for you to challenge yourself with! We like to view these guys as member of our crew and we hope to see you take one home and promote them to your own. Eventually one plant will turn to two and three, trust us with this new addiction because there is no turning back from here.

Floral Friends

Roses are red, violets are blue, plants that bloom? We got em too! One of our goals is to help turn your home into the interior of a Rain Forest Cafe. These pals hold a special place in our hearts because they tend to take more time to maintain but they sure are worth it. Come see more of our selection to find out more about care level!
